A Legacy of Pristine Manufacturing.

For over five decades, ALBA has been the silent backbone of architectural masterpieces. Our journey began with a simple mission: to produce the world’s purest white cement, merging geological precision with sustainable industrial innovation. Today, our heritage is visible in the ivory facades of the world's most iconic structures.
