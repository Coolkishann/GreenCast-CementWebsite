Project Brief: Alba Cement - Architectural Serenity

1. Project Overview

Alba Cement is a premium manufacturer of high-purity white cement, catering to architects and structural engineers who prioritize both aesthetic clarity and structural integrity. This project involves the design and development of a 6-page responsive mobile website that reflects the brand's commitment to "Architectural Serenity."

2. Brand Identity & Visual Language





Aesthetic: Minimalist, modern, and architectural.



Color Palette: 





Primary: Pure White (#FFFFFF)



Accents: Light Green and its shades (representing sustainability and precision).



Surface: Soft grays and off-whites for depth.



Typography: Clean sans-serif (Inter) with generous letter spacing for headings to evoke a premium feel.



Imagery: High-fidelity architectural photography. Product images must have no background to create a floating, integrated effect within the UI.



Motion: Subtle scroll-triggered reveal animations to enhance the premium user experience.

3. Site Map & Core Functionality

The website consists of the following six key screens, optimized for mobile:

A. Home Page





Hero Section: A high-impact carousel featuring architectural structures made with Alba Cement.



Featured Products: A curated preview of the core product line.



Company Introduction: A brief "About" summary.



Client Logos: A "Trusted by Visionary Firms" section.



CTA: Direct paths to product collections or technical consultations.

B. About Us





Heritage: Narrative on the company's legacy in white cement manufacturing.



Core Pillars: Interactive blocks for Purity, Strength, and Sustainability.



Process: A step-by-step visual timeline of precision production (Extraction, Refinement, Calcination).



Leadership: Profiles of the key architects of the corporate strategy.

C. Our Products (Listing)





Filtering: Categories for Construction, Decorative, and Industrial use.



Product Grid: Minimalist cards showcasing products without backgrounds.



Quick Info: High-level value propositions for each variant.

D. Product Detail





Technical Specs: Detailed tables covering Whiteness, Setting Time, and Compressive Strength.



Quality Assurance: Certification badges (ISO, ASTM).



Inspiration Gallery: "Unbound Applications" showing real-world use cases (facades, sculptures, interiors).



CTAs: "Request Sample" and "Download Full Specs."

E. Our Clients





Partnerships: Recognition of collaborative success with top-tier architectural firms.



Case Studies: Featured projects like the "Quartz Pavilion" and "Eco-Villa Series" showcasing the material's versatility.

F. Contact Us





Inquiry Form: Technical inquiry fields for project consultations.



HQ Details: Physical address, technical support lines, and office hours.



Map: A custom-styled map interface for factory and office locations.

4. Technical Constraints




Device: Mobile-first responsive design.



Layout: Single-column vertical flow with consistent margins and gutters.



Performance: Lightweight assets to ensure smooth scroll animations.



Navigation: Sticky TopAppBar and a comprehensive Navigation Drawer.
